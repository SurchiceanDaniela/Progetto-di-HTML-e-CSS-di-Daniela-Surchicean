About me

Hello! I'm Daniela Surchicean, a 26-year-old whirlwind with a passion for Web Development and an illogical obsession with tea. In my world, code and the aroma of tea blend into digital harmony.

My cat, Efea, rules my home with an iron paw, even though she continues to ignore my plea for CSS help. I'm convinced that if only I could teach her to code, we'd have the next four-legged Web phenomenon!

When I'm not causing chaos in the coding world, I immerse myself in fashion because who said a web developer can't be chic? My lines of code are always coordinated with my outfit!

Reading books is my other passion, and I consider myself a true "book devourer." There's no book I can't finish in one night, no matter how many lines of code await me the next day.

So, if you're looking for a web developer with a dash of eccentricity, a touch of elegance, and an incredible passion for coding, I'm here to help take your project to the next level.
